How UNI-PASS Works

Three simple steps to secure authorization.

1. Request

Student submits a digital request via mobile. Includes emergency & lab checks.

2. Approve

Faculty reviews instantly. Long leaves auto-escalate to HOD for final sign-off.

3. Verify

Security guards scan the unique ID at the gate. Green means Go, Red means Stop.

Why Switch to Digital?

100% Paperless

Save thousands of sheets every semester. Environmentally friendly and cost-effective.

Secure Validation

Passes are linked to our central Cloud Database. No fake slips or forged signatures.

Lab Integration

Integrated inventory search helps students request components before applying for On-Duty.
